# Work From Home Policy

**1. Policy Brief and Purpose**

This policy establishes the guidelines, eligibility criteria, expectations, and responsibilities for employees working from home at AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. AkhilaPrime Solutions recognises that flexible work arrangements contribute to employee productivity, wellbeing, and talent retention. This policy ensures that remote work is managed consistently, securely, and in alignment with business needs while maintaining the collaborative culture of the organisation.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all confirmed full-time employees of AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Employees on probation are not eligible for regular WFH arrangements unless specifically approved by HR due to exceptional circumstances. Contract staff and third-party vendors working on AkhilaPrime Solutions premises are not covered under this policy.

**3. Standard Hybrid Work Model**

AkhilaPrime Solutions follows a Hybrid Work Model. All eligible employees are required to be present in the office for a minimum of 3 days per week. The designated office days are Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. The standard work-from-home days are Tuesday and Thursday. Team leads and managers may adjust office or WFH days based on project requirements, client meetings, or sprint cycles with a minimum notice of 48 hours. Employees must not assume that WFH days are fixed and non-negotiable. Business requirements take precedence.

**4. Eligibility Criteria**

To be eligible for the standard WFH arrangement, an employee must have completed their 6-month probation period and received confirmation of employment. The employee must have a performance rating of 3 or above in the most recent appraisal cycle. The employee must have a suitable home workspace with a reliable internet connection of at least 25 Mbps, a dedicated work area free from distractions, and access to required tools and systems. Employees with active Performance Improvement Plans are not eligible for WFH until the PIP is successfully closed.

**5. Extended Remote Work**

In addition to the standard 2 WFH days per week, employees may apply for Extended Remote Work of up to 30 working days per calendar year. Extended remote work may be required for reasons such as relocation within India, medical necessity with doctor's certificate, or care of a dependent family member. The application must be submitted through HRMS with justification and supporting documents at least 10 working days in advance. Approval requires sign-off from the reporting manager and HR Business Partner. Extended remote work beyond 30 days in a year requires additional approval from the department head and HR Director.

**6. Expectations During WFH**

Employees working from home are expected to be available and responsive during standard working hours of 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Core hours of 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM are mandatory for all virtual meetings and collaboration. Employees must attend all scheduled team meetings, stand-ups, and client calls via video with camera turned on unless technically infeasible. Employees must be reachable on AkhilaPrime Solutions' official communication channels including Microsoft Teams, official email, and the HRMS mobile app throughout the workday. Employees must mark WFH attendance on the HRMS portal by 9:15 AM. Failure to mark WFH attendance by 10:00 AM without prior communication will result in a half-day absence. Deliverables, timelines, and quality standards remain unchanged on WFH days. WFH is not a substitute for leave.

**7. Technology and Security Requirements**

Employees working from home must use AkhilaPrime Solutions-issued laptops for all official work. Use of personal devices for accessing company data, systems, or client information is strictly prohibited. Employees must connect to the AkhilaPrime Solutions VPN before accessing any internal systems, databases, or cloud resources. Employees must ensure their home Wi-Fi is password-protected and not shared on public networks. Screens must be locked when stepping away from the workstation. Employees must not work from public places such as cafes, co-working spaces, or airports without using the VPN and ensuring screen privacy. Any security incident or suspected data breach observed during WFH must be reported to the IT Security team immediately at security@akhilaprime.com.

**8. Infrastructure Support**

AkhilaPrime Solutions provides all employees with a company laptop and a USB headset for work-from-home use. A one-time home office setup allowance of Rs. 5,000 is provided to all confirmed employees to purchase ergonomic accessories such as a keyboard, mouse, or laptop stand. This is a reimbursable expense with bill submission required within 60 days of confirmation. Employees are responsible for their own internet connection and power backup. The company does not reimburse internet or electricity costs for standard WFH days.

**9. WFH During Special Circumstances**

In the event of a declared public health emergency, natural disaster, or government-mandated lockdown, AkhilaPrime Solutions may move to full remote operations. During such periods, specific guidelines will be communicated by HR and will supersede this standard policy. Employees must comply with all such emergency guidelines.

**10. Monitoring and Productivity**

Managers are responsible for monitoring team productivity through regular check-ins, task tracking tools such as Jira or Azure DevOps, and weekly deliverable reviews. Employees are expected to maintain the same output quality and quantity on WFH days as in-office days. If a manager observes consistent underperformance correlated with WFH days, they may escalate to HR to review the employee's WFH eligibility.

**11. Revocation of WFH Privilege**

The WFH arrangement is a privilege and not an employee right. It may be revoked entirely or partially in the following circumstances: sustained underperformance during remote work, security incidents caused by negligence during WFH, violation of any clause of this policy, business requirements mandating full in-office presence, or employee being placed on a Performance Improvement Plan. Revocation will be communicated in writing with a minimum of 5 working days notice.

**12. Roles and Responsibilities**

The employee is responsible for maintaining productivity, security compliance, and availability during WFH. The reporting manager is responsible for approving WFH schedules, monitoring output, and flagging issues to HR. HR is responsible for maintaining WFH records, conducting periodic audits, and reviewing eligibility. The IT team is responsible for providing technical support and monitoring VPN compliance.

**13. Policy Review**

This policy is reviewed annually by HR in consultation with IT and senior leadership. Changes will be communicated with 30 days advance notice.

**Acknowledgment**

All employees must acknowledge this policy digitally through the HRMS portal upon joining and upon any revision.
