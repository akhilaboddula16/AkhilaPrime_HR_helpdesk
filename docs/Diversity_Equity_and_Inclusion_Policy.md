# Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Policy

**1. Policy Brief and Purpose**

This policy sets out AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd.'s commitment to building and sustaining a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace. AkhilaPrime Solutions believes that a workforce that reflects a wide range of backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences drives better innovation, stronger team performance, and more empathetic products. Diversity refers to the presence of differences in gender, age, caste, religion, ethnicity, nationality, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, educational background, and socioeconomic background within the organisation. Equity refers to ensuring that systems, processes, and opportunities are fair and that structural barriers are identified and removed. Inclusion refers to creating a culture where every employee feels valued, respected, heard, and able to contribute fully. This policy defines AkhilaPrime Solutions' commitments and the responsibilities of all employees in upholding these values.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and interns of AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. and to all employment-related decisions including recruitment, selection, onboarding, promotion, compensation, learning and development, and separation.

**3. Our Commitments**

AkhilaPrime Solutions makes the following commitments under this policy.

**3.1. Equal Opportunity Employment**

AkhilaPrime Solutions is an equal opportunity employer. All employment decisions are made solely on the basis of merit, qualifications, and demonstrated performance. No employee or candidate will be discriminated against on the basis of gender, age, caste, religion, race, ethnicity, nationality, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, disability, marital status, pregnancy, or any other characteristic protected under applicable Indian law including the Constitution of India and the Equal Remuneration Act.

**3.2. Inclusive Hiring**

AkhilaPrime Solutions is committed to building diverse hiring pipelines. Job descriptions are written using inclusive language and avoid unnecessary requirements that could discourage qualified candidates from underrepresented groups from applying. Interview panels are encouraged to include members of diverse backgrounds wherever possible. Structured interview processes with standardised evaluation criteria are used to reduce unconscious bias in selection decisions.

**3.3. Pay Equity**

AkhilaPrime Solutions is committed to equal pay for equal work. Compensation decisions are made based on the employee's band, role, skills, and performance, and are not influenced by gender, caste, religion, or any other protected characteristic. HR conducts an annual pay equity audit to identify and address unjustified pay gaps across demographic groups. Findings of the audit are reviewed by the HR Director and the management team.

**3.4. Accessible Workplace**

AkhilaPrime Solutions is committed to making reasonable accommodations for employees with disabilities to enable them to perform their roles effectively. Employees who require workplace adjustments may request them through hr@akhilaprime.com. Requests will be reviewed and responded to within 10 working days. The company will not penalise any employee for requesting an accommodation.

**3.5. Inclusive Culture**

AkhilaPrime Solutions expects all employees to treat their colleagues with dignity and respect regardless of any personal characteristic. Discriminatory remarks, jokes, stereotyping, or any behaviour that creates a hostile or unwelcoming environment for any employee are strictly prohibited and will be treated as misconduct under the Disciplinary Policy.

**4. Anti-Discrimination and Anti-Harassment**

Discrimination and harassment on the basis of any protected characteristic are prohibited at AkhilaPrime Solutions. This includes direct discrimination, indirect discrimination through seemingly neutral policies that disproportionately disadvantage a protected group, and harassment in the form of unwanted conduct that violates a person's dignity or creates an intimidating, hostile, degrading, or offensive environment. Sexual harassment is separately addressed in the POSH Policy and investigated by the Internal Complaints Committee. All other forms of discrimination and harassment are addressed through the Grievance Redressal Policy and may escalate to the Disciplinary Policy if substantiated.

**5. DEI Governance**

AkhilaPrime Solutions establishes the following governance mechanisms to advance its DEI commitments. The DEI Committee is a cross-functional committee comprising HR leadership, employee representatives from diverse backgrounds, and a senior sponsor from the leadership team. The committee meets quarterly to review DEI metrics, assess progress, identify barriers, and recommend initiatives. DEI metrics including gender representation by band, hiring source diversity, promotion rates across demographic groups, and inclusion survey scores are tracked by HR and reported to the leadership team annually. AkhilaPrime Solutions conducts an annual Employee Inclusion Survey to measure how included employees feel across different dimensions. Results are shared with all employees with transparency, and action plans are developed in response to identified gaps.

**6. Inclusive Leadership Expectations**

Managers at AkhilaPrime Solutions are expected to model and champion inclusive behaviour. Specifically, managers must ensure that all team members have equitable access to high-visibility projects, mentoring, and growth opportunities. Managers must actively listen to and amplify diverse perspectives in team discussions. Managers must call out and address exclusionary behaviour when they observe it. Managers must complete DEI training as part of their ongoing leadership development. Inclusive leadership behaviour is included as a criterion in the performance review of all managers at Band 4 and above.

**7. DEI Initiatives**

AkhilaPrime Solutions supports the following ongoing DEI initiatives. The Women in Tech Programme includes mentoring, networking, and leadership development opportunities specifically for women at AkhilaPrime Solutions. Return to Work Programme supports individuals who have taken a career break of 1 year or more to re-enter the workforce through structured re-onboarding, mentoring, and flexible working arrangements for the first 6 months. Diversity Hiring Partnerships include partnerships with organisations that support employment of persons with disabilities, LGBTQIA+ individuals, and candidates from underrepresented communities. Inclusive celebrations include acknowledging and celebrating a diverse range of cultural, religious, and community events throughout the year in an inclusive and respectful manner.

**8. Reporting Concerns**

Employees who experience or witness discrimination, harassment, or exclusionary behaviour may raise a concern through the following channels. For informal resolution, the employee may speak to their HR Business Partner or any member of the DEI Committee. For formal resolution, the employee may submit a written complaint through the Grievance Redressal Policy process. For anonymous reporting, the employee may use the ethics reporting channel at ethics@akhilaprime.com. All reports are treated with confidentiality and investigated fairly. Retaliation against anyone who raises a DEI concern in good faith is strictly prohibited and will be treated as misconduct under the Disciplinary Policy.

**9. Roles and Responsibilities**

Every employee is responsible for treating colleagues with respect, contributing to an inclusive team culture, and speaking up when they witness exclusionary behaviour. Managers are responsible for modelling inclusive leadership, ensuring equitable opportunities for their team, and addressing bias and exclusion promptly. The DEI Committee is responsible for setting the DEI agenda, tracking progress, and driving initiatives. The HR Director is responsible for embedding DEI principles in all HR processes including recruitment, promotion, and compensation. The leadership team is responsible for visibly championing DEI at the organisational level and allocating resources to DEI initiatives.

**10. Policy Review**

This policy is reviewed annually by the DEI Committee and HR. Any amendments will be communicated to all employees with 30 days advance notice.

**Acknowledgment**

All employees must acknowledge this policy through the HRMS portal upon joining and upon any subsequent revision.
