# Employee Code of Conduct Policy

**1. Policy Brief and Purpose**

This Code of Conduct Policy defines the standards of professional behaviour, ethics, and integrity expected from every employee of AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. It serves as a guide for making decisions that are consistent with the company's values of integrity, respect, accountability, and excellence. Adherence to this policy is a condition of employment and applies at all times during the course of work, whether on company premises, at client sites, or while representing AkhilaPrime Solutions in any capacity.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all employees of AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. including permanent, probationary, and contractual staff, as well as interns, across all office locations and remote work settings.

**3. Professional Behaviour**

**3.1. Workplace Conduct**

All employees are expected to behave in a professional, courteous, and respectful manner toward colleagues, clients, vendors, and all other stakeholders at all times. Employees must maintain a positive and collaborative attitude in team interactions. Disruptive, aggressive, or threatening behaviour in the workplace will not be tolerated. Employees must maintain personal hygiene and dress in a manner consistent with the professional environment. The standard dress code is business casual from Monday to Thursday and casual on Fridays. Smart attire is mandatory on client visit days regardless of the day of the week.

**3.2. Communication Standards**

Employees must use professional language in all official communications including emails, messaging platforms, and meetings. Offensive, derogatory, or discriminatory language based on gender, caste, religion, age, sexual orientation, disability, or ethnicity is strictly prohibited. All external communications representing AkhilaPrime Solutions must be reviewed and aligned with the company's brand guidelines before sending.

**4. Confidentiality and Data Protection**

Employees have access to sensitive and confidential information including client data, proprietary software, business strategies, financial records, and employee personal data. All such information must be treated as strictly confidential. Employees must not disclose confidential information to unauthorised internal or external parties during or after their employment. Employees must sign and abide by the Non-Disclosure Agreement provided at the time of joining. Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices, shared via personal email, or accessed outside of secure AkhilaPrime Solutions systems without VPN. Violation of data confidentiality may result in immediate termination and legal action.

**5. Conflict of Interest**

Employees must avoid situations where their personal interests conflict with the interests of AkhilaPrime Solutions. A conflict of interest arises when an employee has a financial interest in or relationship with a competitor, client, or vendor that could influence their professional judgment. Employees must disclose any potential conflict of interest to HR in writing as soon as they become aware of it. Employees must not use their position at AkhilaPrime Solutions to gain personal financial benefit beyond their standard compensation. Family members or close personal relationships of an employee may not be hired, promoted, or awarded contracts where the employee has direct decision-making authority.

**6. Intellectual Property**

All work products, software, code, designs, reports, analyses, and other outputs created by an employee in the course of their employment at AkhilaPrime Solutions are the sole intellectual property of the company. Employees may not use, reproduce, sell, or claim ownership of any such work product outside of their employment. Open-source tools or third-party software integrated into company products must be done only with IT team approval and in compliance with applicable licensing agreements.

**7. Use of Company Assets**

Company assets including laptops, mobile phones, software licences, office equipment, and infrastructure must be used solely for official business purposes. Employees must not use company assets for personal business, freelance projects, or any activity that competes with AkhilaPrime Solutions. All company equipment must be maintained with reasonable care and returned in good condition upon separation. Damage to company assets caused by negligence will be recovered from the employee's final settlement.

**8. Internet and Social Media Use**

Employees are permitted to use the internet during work hours for business-related purposes only. Personal social media browsing, online shopping, streaming, or gaming on company networks during work hours is not permitted. Employees may use personal devices for personal browsing during designated break times. Employees must not post comments, images, or content on social media that disparages AkhilaPrime Solutions, its clients, employees, or leadership. Employees must not share confidential company information, client names, project details, or financial data on any public platform. Violations will be treated as a serious breach of conduct.

**9. Anti-Bribery and Corruption**

AkhilaPrime Solutions maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward bribery and corruption in all forms. Employees must not offer, give, solicit, or accept bribes, kickbacks, or any form of improper payment or gift intended to gain business advantage. Gifts from vendors or clients of a value exceeding Rs. 1,000 must be declared to HR. Employees who discover or suspect bribery or corruption must report it immediately through the Whistleblower mechanism at ethics@akhilaprime.com.

**10. Substance Use**

Employees must not report to work or attend any work-related event under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances. Possession, consumption, or distribution of illegal substances on company premises or at any company-sponsored event is strictly prohibited. Violation of this rule will result in immediate disciplinary action including possible termination.

**11. Attendance and Punctuality**

Employees are expected to maintain regular attendance and be punctual as defined in the Attendance Policy. Habitual absenteeism, leaving early without approval, or failure to meet attendance standards without valid reason is a violation of this policy.

**12. Whistleblower Protection**

Employees who report genuine concerns about unethical behaviour, policy violations, financial irregularities, or legal non-compliance are protected under this policy. Reports can be made anonymously through the ethics helpline at ethics@akhilaprime.com or in writing to the HR Director. Employees who raise concerns in good faith will not face any retaliation. False reports made with malicious intent will be subject to disciplinary action.

**13. Disciplinary Action**

Violations of this Code of Conduct will be investigated and may result in disciplinary action. Minor violations may result in a verbal or written warning. Moderate violations may result in suspension without pay, demotion, or performance improvement plans. Serious violations such as data theft, fraud, harassment, or bribery will result in immediate termination and may be referred to law enforcement authorities.

**14. Policy Review**

This policy is reviewed annually by HR and the Legal team. Amendments will be communicated with 30 days advance notice.

**Acknowledgment**

All employees must acknowledge this policy digitally through the HRMS portal upon joining and upon any subsequent revision.
