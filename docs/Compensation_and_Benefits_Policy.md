# Compensation and Benefits Policy

**1. Policy Brief and Purpose**

This policy describes the compensation philosophy, salary structure, benefits entitlements, and reward programmes at AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. The objective of this policy is to attract, retain, and motivate talented professionals by offering competitive and equitable compensation aligned with industry benchmarks, individual performance, and organisational capability.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all full-time permanent employees of AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Contractual staff compensation is governed by their respective contracts. Interns receive a stipend as agreed at the time of selection and are not covered under this policy.

**3. Compensation Philosophy**

AkhilaPrime Solutions benchmarks its compensation against the 75th percentile of the Indian IT industry pay range as assessed through annual market surveys conducted with reputed compensation benchmarking firms. The company believes in pay for performance, meaning that high performers are compensated significantly above the market median. All compensation decisions are made free of bias related to gender, caste, religion, or any other protected characteristic. AkhilaPrime Solutions is committed to pay equity and conducts internal pay audits annually.

**4. Salary Structure**

The total Cost to Company (CTC) at AkhilaPrime Solutions is structured as follows. Basic Salary constitutes 40 percent of CTC and is the foundation for all statutory calculations including Provident Fund, Gratuity, and leave encashment. House Rent Allowance (HRA) constitutes 20 percent of CTC and is provided to support employee housing costs. Special Allowance constitutes 20 percent of CTC and is a flexible component paid monthly. Variable Pay constitutes 15 percent of CTC and is paid annually in April based on individual performance rating as described in the Performance Review Policy. Employer Provident Fund contribution constitutes 5 percent of CTC and is remitted to the Employees Provident Fund Organisation as per the EPF Act 1952.

**5. Salary Disbursement**

Salaries are credited to the employee's designated bank account on the last working day of each month. Salary slips are generated and made available on the HRMS portal by the 5th of the following month. Employees must ensure their bank account details and PAN card information in HRMS are accurate and up to date. In case of salary discrepancies, employees must raise a query with the Finance team through the HRMS helpdesk within 5 working days of salary credit.

**6. Provident Fund**

All employees drawing a basic salary up to Rs. 15,000 per month are mandatorily covered under the Employees Provident Fund and Miscellaneous Provisions Act 1952. Employees drawing basic above Rs. 15,000 are also enrolled in the EPF scheme as per AkhilaPrime Solutions' policy of universal PF coverage. The employee contributes 12 percent of basic salary to EPF and the employer contributes an equal amount. Of the employer contribution, 8.33 percent goes to the Employee Pension Scheme (EPS) and the balance to the EPF account. Employees can access their EPF passbook through the EPFO member portal using their UAN number. UAN activation assistance is provided by HR during onboarding.

**7. Gratuity**

Employees who have completed 5 or more years of continuous service are entitled to gratuity under the Payment of Gratuity Act 1972. Gratuity is paid at the time of separation from the company. The calculation is: Gratuity equals Last Basic Salary divided by 26 multiplied by 15 multiplied by number of completed years of service. AkhilaPrime Solutions maintains a group gratuity policy with LIC of India to ensure gratuity payments are funded.

**8. Health Insurance**

AkhilaPrime Solutions provides group health insurance coverage to all employees and their dependents. The sum insured under the group mediclaim policy is Rs. 5 lakh per employee per annum. Coverage includes the employee, spouse or partner, up to 2 dependent children below 25 years of age, and dependent parents or parents-in-law subject to an additional premium paid by the employee. Pre-existing conditions are covered from day one of joining. Maternity expenses are covered up to Rs. 75,000 per delivery. Dental and vision care are not included in the group policy. Employees may opt for a top-up cover of Rs. 5 lakh at an additional premium deducted from their salary. The HR team coordinates all insurance-related queries through the designated insurance broker.

**9. Term Life Insurance**

All employees are covered under a group term life insurance policy. The sum assured is 3 times the annual CTC of the employee. This cover is provided free of cost by the company and requires no employee contribution. Nominees must be registered through the HRMS portal. In the event of death of an employee, the nominee will receive the sum assured in addition to any other statutory dues.

**10. Employee Provident Fund Insurance**

Under the Employees Deposit Linked Insurance (EDLI) scheme administered by EPFO, all EPF members are covered for a life insurance benefit of up to Rs. 7 lakh. This is administered directly through EPFO and does not require any additional registration.

**11. Flexible Benefits Plan**

Employees at Band 4 and above are eligible to participate in the Flexible Benefits Plan which allows them to restructure up to 20 percent of their special allowance component into tax-efficient reimbursement heads such as Leave Travel Allowance, Meal Coupons, Fuel and Vehicle Maintenance Allowance, Professional Development Allowance, and Books and Periodicals Allowance. FBP elections must be made at the beginning of the financial year in April through the HRMS portal and cannot be changed mid-year except in cases of life events such as marriage or birth of a child.

**12. National Pension System**

Employees who wish to build additional retirement savings may voluntarily enrol in the National Pension System through AkhilaPrime Solutions' corporate NPS scheme. AkhilaPrime Solutions contributes an additional 10 percent of basic salary to the employee's NPS account as a retention benefit for employees who opt in and have completed 2 years of service. NPS contributions are eligible for additional tax deductions under Section 80CCD(2) of the Income Tax Act.

**13. Referral Bonus**

Employees who refer candidates for open positions at AkhilaPrime Solutions are eligible for a referral bonus. The referral bonus is paid in two tranches: 50 percent upon the referred employee completing 3 months of service and 50 percent upon completion of 6 months. Referral bonus amounts by grade are: Rs. 20,000 for non-technical roles, Rs. 30,000 for technical individual contributor roles, Rs. 50,000 for senior and lead roles, and Rs. 75,000 for managerial and specialist roles. Referrals must be submitted through the HRMS Referral module before the candidate applies.

**14. Salary Revision**

Annual salary revisions are effective from 1st April and are based on individual performance ratings as described in the Performance Review Policy. Off-cycle salary revisions may be considered in exceptional circumstances such as counter-offers, significant role expansion, or market correction and require HR Director and Finance approval.

**15. Payroll Deductions**

The following statutory deductions are made from the employee's gross salary each month: Employee PF contribution at 12 percent of basic salary; Professional Tax as per the applicable state rate; Income Tax deducted at source (TDS) as per the employee's declared tax regime under the Income Tax Act 1961. Employees must submit their tax declarations and investment proofs through the HRMS portal between January and February each year. Failure to submit proofs will result in deduction of tax at the maximum marginal rate for the remaining months.

**16. Roles and Responsibilities**

The Finance and Payroll team is responsible for accurate and timely salary processing, statutory compliance, and generating salary slips. HR is responsible for compensation benchmarking, policy administration, and addressing compensation queries. Employees are responsible for maintaining accurate personal and bank details in HRMS, submitting tax declarations on time, and flagging discrepancies promptly.

**17. Policy Review**

This policy is reviewed annually by Finance and HR in consultation with the compensation benchmarking data and leadership. Revisions will be communicated with 30 days advance notice.

**Acknowledgment**

All employees must acknowledge this policy through the HRMS portal upon joining and upon any revision.
