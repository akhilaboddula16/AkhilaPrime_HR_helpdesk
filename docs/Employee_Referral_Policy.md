# Employee Referral Policy

**1. Policy Brief and Purpose**

This policy defines the Employee Referral Programme at AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd., which enables employees to recommend qualified candidates from their network for open positions within the company. AkhilaPrime Solutions values referrals as a high-quality, cost-effective source of talent and believes that employees are best placed to identify individuals whose skills and cultural fit align with the organisation. This policy defines eligibility, the referral process, bonus amounts, payment timelines, and the conditions under which referral bonuses are paid or withheld.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all confirmed full-time permanent employees of AkhilaPrime Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Probationary employees, contractual staff, interns, and consultants are not eligible to receive referral bonuses. The hiring managers and HR team members directly involved in the recruitment process for a specific role are not eligible to receive a referral bonus for that particular role, as they are already part of the selection decision.

**3. Roles Open for Referral**

All open positions at AkhilaPrime Solutions are eligible for employee referrals unless explicitly marked otherwise. Positions marked as confidential or executive search, typically Band 8 and above, may be excluded from the referral programme at HR's discretion. Open positions are listed on the internal job board at hrms.akhilaprime.com under the Careers section, which is updated in real time by the Talent Acquisition team.

**4. Referral Bonus Structure**

The referral bonus is a one-time payment made to the referring employee upon the referred candidate successfully completing the retention period defined in Section 6. The bonus amounts are as follows by the band of the role being filled. For roles at Band 1 to Band 2, the referral bonus is Rs. 15,000. For roles at Band 3 to Band 4, the referral bonus is Rs. 30,000. For roles at Band 5 to Band 6, the referral bonus is Rs. 50,000. For roles at Band 7 and above, the referral bonus is Rs. 75,000. For niche or hard-to-fill roles designated as Priority Referral roles by the Talent Acquisition team, the referral bonus is enhanced by 50 percent over the standard rate. Priority Referral roles are listed separately on the internal job board and are updated quarterly. All referral bonuses are subject to applicable income tax deductions as per the Income Tax Act and are included in the referring employee's Form 16.

**5. How to Submit a Referral**

Step 1 is identifying an open role. The employee identifies a suitable open position on the HRMS internal job board and ensures the referred candidate meets the minimum qualifications listed. Step 2 is submitting the referral. The employee submits the referral through the HRMS Referral Module at hrms.akhilaprime.com before or at the same time as the referred candidate applies. The referral submission must include the candidate's full name, email address, phone number, current employer if applicable, and a brief note on why the employee is recommending this candidate. Self-referrals and referrals submitted after the candidate has already applied independently are not eligible for a referral bonus. Step 3 is candidate application. The referred candidate must apply for the open position through the official careers portal at careers.akhilaprime.com. The referral link in the system will automatically associate the application with the referring employee. Step 4 is the recruitment process. The referred candidate goes through the standard recruitment process including screening, technical assessment, and interviews. The referring employee is not involved in the selection or interview process. Step 5 is the outcome. The Talent Acquisition team notifies the referring employee of the outcome, whether the candidate was selected or not, within 5 working days of the final hiring decision.

**6. Referral Bonus Payment Timeline**

The referral bonus is paid in two equal tranches to balance incentive with retention. The first tranche of 50 percent of the bonus is paid in the payroll cycle following the completion of 3 months of continuous employment by the referred candidate from their date of joining. The second tranche of 50 percent of the bonus is paid in the payroll cycle following the completion of 6 months of continuous employment by the referred candidate. If the referred candidate leaves AkhilaPrime Solutions voluntarily or is terminated before completing 3 months, no bonus is paid to the referring employee. If the referred candidate leaves or is terminated after completing 3 months but before completing 6 months, only the first tranche is paid and the second tranche is forfeited.

**7. Conditions and Exclusions**

The following conditions apply to all referral bonus eligibility. The referring employee must be in active employment with AkhilaPrime Solutions at the time each bonus tranche is due. If the referring employee has resigned or been terminated before a tranche is due, that tranche is forfeited. The referred candidate must be a genuinely new candidate not previously employed by AkhilaPrime Solutions or not previously rejected within the last 12 months for the same or similar role. The referring employee must not be a relative of the referred candidate. Referral of immediate family members including spouse, parent, sibling, or child is not permitted under this policy. Referring a candidate through unofficial or verbal channels without submitting through the HRMS Referral Module disqualifies the referral from bonus eligibility. Multiple employees who refer the same candidate for the same role will share the bonus equally only if both referrals were submitted through HRMS before the candidate applied. If only one referral was submitted through the proper channel, only that employee is eligible for the bonus.

**8. Candidate Experience and Fair Process**

Referred candidates receive the same recruitment experience as direct applicants. They are assessed on merit alone. Referral status does not guarantee an interview or an offer. HR and the Talent Acquisition team are responsible for ensuring no preferential treatment is given to referred candidates beyond the standard internal process. The referring employee must not coach the referred candidate in answering assessment or interview questions specific to AkhilaPrime Solutions' evaluation criteria, as this compromises the integrity of the process and may result in disqualification of both the referral and the bonus.

**9. Tracking and Transparency**

Referring employees can track the status of their referral at any stage through the HRMS Referral Module. Status updates visible to the referring employee include: referral received, candidate applied, under review, interview stage, offer made, offer accepted, and joined. Referring employees may contact the Talent Acquisition team at talent@akhilaprime.com for queries about their referral status.

**10. Disputes and Appeals**

If a referring employee believes they have been incorrectly excluded from a referral bonus, they may raise the matter with the HR Business Partner in writing within 30 days of the relevant payout date. HR will review the case and respond within 10 working days. The HR Director's decision on referral bonus disputes is final.

**11. Roles and Responsibilities**

The employee is responsible for submitting referrals through HRMS before the candidate applies, providing accurate information, and complying with the conditions of this policy. The Talent Acquisition team is responsible for maintaining the referral module, tracking referral outcomes, communicating updates to referring employees, and processing bonus payouts through payroll. The HR Business Partner is responsible for resolving referral disputes and ensuring the policy is applied consistently.

**12. Policy Review**

This policy is reviewed annually by HR and the Talent Acquisition team. Bonus amounts may be revised as part of the annual review. Any amendments will be communicated to all employees with 30 days advance notice.

**Acknowledgment**

All employees must acknowledge this policy through the HRMS portal upon joining and upon any subsequent revision.
